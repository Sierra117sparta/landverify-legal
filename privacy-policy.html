Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to LandVerify and is intended to describe how user information is collected, processed, stored, shared, and protected when users access the app, sign in using Google, submit property verification requests, upload documents, use map-based property selection, make payments, and receive reports.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how J Aryavarta Tech Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal data when you use the LandVerify mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service.

2. Who We Are

J Aryavarta Tech Private Limited is based in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India, and is the operator and developer of the LandVerify platform.

We provide technology-enabled land and property history verification services, including coordination with advocates, legal professionals, clerical personnel, and other service providers to help users understand ownership history, legal liabilities, encumbrances, disputes, and related issues affecting land and constructed properties.

3. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data we collect from users who access the Services, sign in using Google Sign-In, submit verification requests, upload documents, use map-based property selection, make payments, communicate with us, receive reports through the platform, or request support, correction, deletion, or grievance handling.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, applications, or services that may be linked to or integrated with the Services, and such third parties are governed by their own privacy practices.

4. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and contact information, such as your name, mobile number, email address, and any other contact details you provide.
  • If you sign in using Google Sign-In or similar authentication methods, we may receive your name, email address, account identifier, and other basic profile information made available by that provider based on your permissions and the scopes used. We do not intentionally collect or store your Google profile photo unless specifically enabled in a future version of the Services.
  • Authentication and session data, including login tokens, refresh tokens, session identifiers, and related security metadata required to maintain secure access to your account and Services.
  • Property and location-related information, including state, city, address, pincode, khasra number, survey number, tehsil, village, and other identifying property details submitted for verification.
  • Map and location assistance data. Where you grant location permission while using the app, the app may access device location to help load the map interface, improve property search, and assist in selecting the relevant property on the map. However, we primarily use and store the property address, property search result, and map selection details submitted by you for verification purposes, and we do not state that we store continuous background location history.
  • User-provided files, documents, images, scans, and related records uploaded by you, including sale deeds, title documents, tax receipts, revenue records, court papers, approvals, NOCs, and other supporting materials relevant to verification. These uploads may contain personal data relating to you or third parties.
  • Additional notes or statements voluntarily provided by you regarding ownership, possession, disputes, family arrangements, transaction history, or concerns associated with the property.
  • Transaction and payment-related data, such as payment status, verification ticket or order number, transaction reference numbers, payment IDs, order identifiers, amount paid, and billing-related details received from our payment partners. We do not store full card, net banking, or UPI credential details.
  • Technical and usage data, such as device type, operating system, app version, IP address, log records, access times, error traces, masked security logs, and service usage information generated when you use the Services.
  • Internal verification records, work notes, admin review records, lawyer or advocate coordination records, and reports generated by us or on our behalf during the course of providing the Services.

5. How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from you when you create an account, sign in using Google or other sign-in services, submit a verification request, fill in property details, upload documents, provide location access, search or select a property on the map, make a payment, contact us, or otherwise interact with the Services.

We may also collect information automatically through app permissions, device logs, security monitoring, session handling tools, and technical integrations necessary for authentication, security, operations, and service improvement.

In addition, we may collect or verify information from third-party or public sources, including government departments, revenue offices, registry systems, courts, and other lawful sources relevant to property due diligence.

6. Purpose of Use

We use your information to identify and verify the relevant property, authenticate your account, maintain sessions, process your verification request, coordinate with advocates and service providers, generate and deliver reports, process payments, communicate with you, prevent fraud, maintain platform security, comply with law, and improve our Services.

7. Legal Basis and Consent

We process personal data primarily on the basis of your consent and for lawful purposes connected with provision of the Services, fraud prevention, security, authentication, and compliance with applicable Indian laws.

Where consent is required, it will be obtained at or before the time of collection. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us, but such withdrawal may affect our ability to continue providing some or all of the Services.

8. Confidentiality and Professional Handling

We treat information and documents submitted for verification as confidential and share them only on a need-to-know basis with our internal team and with advocates, legal professionals, field personnel, clerical staff, consultants, and service providers engaged for the purpose of carrying out the verification.

We take reasonable steps to ensure that persons handling such data are subject to confidentiality obligations, professional responsibilities, or contractual safeguards appropriate to the nature of the Services.

9. Data Sharing

We may share your information with advocates and verification professionals, technology and infrastructure providers, hosting and authentication providers, payment and communication partners, email delivery providers, professional advisors, and government or regulatory authorities where required by law or reasonably necessary for verification.

We may use third-party service providers including Railway for backend infrastructure and database hosting, Vercel for the admin portal and related deployment infrastructure, Google services for authentication and map functionality, Razorpay for payment processing, and Gmail or Zoho Mail or similar services for sending reports, updates, and communications.

We do not sell your personal data for monetary consideration.

10. Admin Access and External Professional Sharing

Uploaded documents and related verification materials may be accessed by authorised internal administrators through a role-based admin portal where necessary to review submissions, verify records, and prepare findings.

External advocates or third-party professionals engaged for verification do not necessarily receive direct access to the admin portal. Where necessary, relevant document information, extracts, or supporting materials may instead be shared with them through controlled communication channels such as email for the limited purpose of verification and legal review.

11. Third-Party and Public Sources

The Services may rely on records, extracts, or information obtained from government offices, registries, courts, public databases, and other third-party sources. Such information may be incomplete, delayed, inconsistent, inaccessible, or subject to later change.

Accordingly, while we make reasonable efforts in the verification process, we do not guarantee the absolute accuracy, completeness, or ongoing validity of third-party or public-source information.

12. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational safeguards designed to protect your data against unauthorised access, disclosure, misuse, alteration, or destruction. These measures may include role-based access controls, secure hosting, restricted internal access, encryption in transit where appropriate, monitoring, masked or minimised logging practices, and administrative safeguards for document handling.

However, no digital platform or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and we cannot promise absolute security.

13. Data Retention

We retain personal data and verification-related records only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, comply with legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

After the relevant retention period, we may delete, archive, or anonymise data in accordance with applicable law and internal policies.

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate or incomplete information, deletion of data where legally permissible, withdrawal of consent, and grievance redressal in relation to the processing of your information.

To request access, correction, deletion, or account-related privacy assistance, please email infojaryavartatech@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject line “Privacy Request”. We may require reasonable verification of your identity before acting on any such request.
