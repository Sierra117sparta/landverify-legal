Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how J Aryavarta Tech Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal data when you use the LandVerify mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service.

2. Who We Are

J Aryavarta Tech Private Limited is based in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India, and is the operator and developer of the LandVerify platform.

We provide technology-enabled land and property history verification services, including coordination with advocates, legal professionals, clerical personnel, and other service providers to help users understand ownership history, legal liabilities, encumbrances, disputes, and related issues affecting land and constructed properties.

3. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data we collect from users who access the Services, submit verification requests, upload documents, make payments, communicate with us, or receive reports through the platform.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, applications, or services that may be linked to or integrated with the Services, and such third parties are governed by their own privacy practices.

4. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and contact information, such as your name, mobile number, email address, and any other contact details you provide.
  • Property and location information, including GPS coordinates captured through the in-app map, state, city, address, pincode, khasra number, survey number, tehsil, village, and other identifying property details.
  • Property classification details, such as whether the property is land, apartment, villa, residential, commercial, or agricultural, and the nature of the land or property.
  • Documents and related records uploaded by you, including sale deeds, title documents, tax receipts, revenue records, court papers, approvals, NOCs, and other supporting materials relevant to verification.
  • Additional notes or statements voluntarily provided by you regarding ownership, possession, disputes, family arrangements, transaction history, or concerns associated with the property.
  • Transaction and payment data, such as payment status, transaction reference numbers, order identifiers, amount paid, and billing-related details received from our payment partners. We do not store full card, net banking, or UPI credential details.
  • Technical and usage data, such as device type, operating system, app version, IP address, log records, access times, crash reports, and analytics information generated when you use the Services.
  • Internal verification records and reports generated by us or on our behalf during the course of providing the Services.

5. How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from you when you create an account, submit a verification request, fill in property details, upload documents, provide location access, make a payment, contact us, or otherwise interact with the Services.

We may also collect information automatically through app permissions, device logs, analytics tools, and technical integrations necessary for security, operations, and service improvement.

In addition, we may collect or verify information from third-party or public sources, including government departments, revenue offices, registry systems, courts, and other lawful sources relevant to property due diligence.

6. Purpose of Use

We use your information to identify and verify the relevant property, process your verification request, coordinate with advocates and service providers, generate and deliver reports, process payments, communicate with you, prevent fraud, comply with law, and improve our Services.

7. Legal Basis and Consent

We process personal data primarily on the basis of your consent and for lawful purposes connected with provision of the Services, fraud prevention, security, and compliance with applicable Indian laws.

Where consent is required, it will be obtained at or before the time of collection. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us, but such withdrawal may affect our ability to continue providing some or all of the Services.

8. Confidentiality and Professional Handling

We treat information and documents submitted for verification as confidential and share them only on a need-to-know basis with our internal team and with advocates, legal professionals, field personnel, clerical staff, consultants, and service providers engaged for the purpose of carrying out the verification.

We take reasonable steps to ensure that persons handling such data are subject to confidentiality obligations, professional responsibilities, or contractual safeguards appropriate to the nature of the Services.

9. Data Sharing

We may share your information with advocates and verification professionals, technology and infrastructure providers, payment and communication partners, professional advisors, and government or regulatory authorities where required by law or reasonably necessary for verification.
